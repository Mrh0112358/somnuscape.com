Welcome to Somnuscape ("we", "our", or "us"), accessible at www.somnuscape.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how our website operates. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

Section 01

Who We Are

Somnuscape is a sleep and dream wellness platform offering personalised sleep ritual generation, dream pattern analysis, and free and premium sleep and dream printable resources. The site is operated as an independent website based in the United Kingdom.

For any privacy-related queries please contact us at: hello@somnuscape.com

Section 02

What Information We Collect

Somnuscape is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not require you to create an account, register, or provide any personal information to use our free tools.

Tool inputs: The information you enter into our Sleep Ritual Generator and Dream Pattern Analyser — including your sleep preferences, dream descriptions and lifestyle habits — is sent to the Claude AI API in real time to generate your personalised results. This information is not stored, logged, or retained by us after your result has been generated.

Newsletter signup: If you choose to subscribe to our newsletter we collect your email address solely for the purpose of sending you our monthly content. We do not share your email address with third parties.

Automatically collected data: Like most websites, certain technical information may be automatically collected when you visit, including your IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and time of visit. This data is collected via third-party services described below and is used in aggregate, anonymised form only.

Section 07

Your Rights Under GDPR

As we are based in the United Kingdom and may serve visitors from the European Economic Area, we are committed to upholding your rights under the UK GDPR and EU GDPR. These rights include:

The right to access — you have the right to request information about any personal data we hold about you.

The right to erasure — you have the right to request that we delete any personal data we hold about you.

The right to restrict processing — you have the right to request that we limit how we use your data.

The right to object — you have the right to object to our processing of your personal data.

The right to data portability — you have the right to request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.

As Somnuscape does not store personal data beyond newsletter email addresses and what is described in this policy, most of these rights are satisfied by design. To exercise any of these rights please contact us using the details below.
